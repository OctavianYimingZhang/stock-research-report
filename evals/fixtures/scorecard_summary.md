# Projection Fixture

## Evidence Boundary

This fixture validates the focused scorecard view. The scorecard summarizes
existing objects and creates no new facts [filing].

## Decision Scorecard

The scorecard includes company quality, operating trend, expectation revision,
growth logic, momentum state, valuation odds, risk filter, and action grade.

## Binding Cap Reason

The binding cap reason names the first decision-critical blocker.

## Trade Plan Effect

The final grade translates into position size, observation, trim, add, or
avoidance logic.
