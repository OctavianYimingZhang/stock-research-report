# Projection Fixture

## Scope

This fixture validates the focused source-conflict view. It uses synthetic
placeholders and no material numbers.

## Conflicting Sources

The report identifies conflicting source classes, periods, and metrics.

## Conflict Resolution

Primary filings, regulator records, exchange disclosures, transcripts, and
reliable market data outrank secondary commentary.

## Blocked Conclusions

Unresolved conflicts block affected claims instead of being averaged.
