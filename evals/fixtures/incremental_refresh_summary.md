# Projection Fixture

## New Sources

This fixture validates the focused incremental-refresh view. It uses synthetic
placeholders and no material numbers.

## Changed Claims

Changed claims are tied to new source snapshots and updated evidence IDs.

## Unchanged Claims

Unchanged claims remain stable only when the new source does not affect their
lineage or freshness.

## Revised Conclusions

Revised conclusions identify the changed gate, affected object, and blocked or
unblocked report section.
