# Evaluation Fixtures

These public fixtures check that the stock research skill keeps reports
source-backed, current, conflict-aware, explicit about assumptions, and focused
on a testable alpha thesis rather than a generic compliant report.

Each case describes a user request, expected behavior, and blocker conditions.
The fixtures are synthetic and do not assert live market facts. Alpha-oriented
cases cover mispricing thesis, company control point, demand proxy ladder,
operating machine, revision bridge, catalyst-linked trade plan, and broken
thesis evidence.
